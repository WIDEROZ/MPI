$$C_{0} = n_{0}\cos(\alpha_{0})=n(y)\cos(\alpha(y))$$
$$n(y) = \frac{C_{0}}{\cos(\alpha(y))}$$
$$ds\cos(\alpha(y)) = dx$$
$$\boxed{n(y) = C_{0} \frac{ds}{dx}}$$
$$C_{0}^{2} = n^{2}(y) \left( \frac{ds}{dx} \right)^{2}=n^{2}(y) \left( 1+ \left( \frac{dy}{dx} \right)^{2} \right)$$
