#### (b).
Soit $P \in \mathrm{Ker}(\varphi)$, 
$$X^{2}P'(X) - XP(X) = 0$$
$$X^{2}P'(X) = XP(X)$$
Soit $x \in \mathbb{R}^{*}$, 
$$xP'(x) = P(x)$$
$$P(x) = \sum_{k \in \mathbb{N}} \lambda_{k}x^{k}$$
$$P'(x) = \sum_{k \in \mathbb{N}^{*}}k\lambda_{k}x^{k-1}$$
$$xP'(x) = \sum_{k \in \mathbb{N}^{*}}k\lambda_{k}x^{k}=\sum_{k \in \mathbb{N}} \lambda_{k} x^{k} = P(x)$$
