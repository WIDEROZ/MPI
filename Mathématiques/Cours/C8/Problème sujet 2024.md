### Question 8
$$(x_{1}, x_{2})\begin{pmatrix}
2&1 \\
1&1 
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
x_{1} \\
x_{2}
\end{pmatrix} = (x_{1}, x_{2})\begin{pmatrix}
2x_{1}+x_{2} \\
x_{1}+x_{2}
\end{pmatrix} =
2x_{1}^{2} + 2x_{1}x_{2} + x_{2}^{2}
$$
$$= (x_{1}+x_{2})^{2} + x_{1}^{2} > 0$$
car $x_{1} \neq 0$
