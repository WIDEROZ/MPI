## Équation d'Alembert
$$\boxed{\Delta(s) - \frac{1}{c^{2}}\frac{\partial^{2}s}{\partial t^{2}}=0 }$$
$$\boxed{\vec{\Delta}(\vec{A}) - \frac{1}{c^{2}}\frac{\partial^{2}\vec{A}}{\partial t^{2}}=0 }$$
