\mathcal{M}(\vec{P}) = (mgD(\cos(\theta)\vec{e}_{x} +\sin(\theta) \vec{e}_{y})\wedge \vec{e}_{x}).\vec{e}_{z} = -mgD\sin(\theta)
$$
