On injecte :
$$\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2m} \frac{\partial^{2}\psi}{\partial x^{2}} = -\frac{\hbar^{2}}{2m}k_{0}^{2} A e^{ i(k_{0}x-\omega_{0}t) }$$
$$-i\hbar \frac{\partial \psi}{\partial t}  = -\hbar \omega_{0} A e^{ i(k_{0}x-\omega_{0}t) }$$
